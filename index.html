Workout for men

Day 1: Upper Body (Push & Pull)

Barbell Bench Press – 4x6-8 (Strength & mass)

Pull-Ups (Weighted if strong enough) – 4x6-10 (Back & biceps)

Overhead Press (Barbell or Dumbbell) – 3x8-10 (Shoulders)

Barbell-row Rows – 3x8-12 (Back thickness)

Dips (Weighted if possible) – 3x8-12 (Chest & triceps)

Lateral Raises – 3x12-15 (Shoulder width)

>

Face Pulls – 3x12-15 (Shoulder health)

Day 2: Lower Body (Legs & Glutes)

Barbell Squats – 4x6-8 (Quads & overall leg strength)

Deadlifts (Conventional or Romanian) – 4x6 (Full posterior chain)

Bulgarian Split Squats – 3x8-12 (Leg & glute activation)

Hip Thrusts – 3x10-12 (Glute growth)

Seated Hamstring Curls – 3x12-15 (Hamstring isolation)

Calf Raises – 3x12-15 (Optional)

Day 4: Upper Body (Push & Pull - Variation)

Incline Dumbbell Press – 4x8-10 (Upper chest focus)

Barbell Row – 4x8-10 (Back thickness)

Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3x8-12 (Shoulders)

Chin-Ups (Weighted if strong enough) – 3x6-10 (Biceps & lats)

EZ Bar Bicep Curls – 3x12-15 (Biceps pump)

Triceps Rope Pushdowns – 3x12-15 (Triceps)

>

Face Pulls – 3x12-15 (Posture & injury prevention)

Day 5: Lower Body (Legs & Glutes - Variation)

Deadlifts (Conventional or Romanian) – 4x6 (Full posterior chain)

Leg Press – 3x10-12 (Quad focus)

Bulgarian Split Squats – 3x8-12 (Leg & glute activation)

Hip Thrusts – 3x10-12 (Glute growth)

Seated Hamstring Curls – 3x12-15 (Hamstring isolation)

Calf Raises – 3x12-15 (Optional)
