Calf Raises Guide
1. Stand with the balls of your feet on an elevated surface, keeping your heels off the edge.
2. Push through the balls of your feet to raise your heels as high as possible.
3. Squeeze your calves at the top position and hold for a second.
4. Slowly lower your heels back to the starting position and repeat.
