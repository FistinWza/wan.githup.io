Pull Ups Guide
1. Grab the pull-up bar with an overhand grip, slightly wider than shoulder-width.
2. Engage your core and pull yourself up until your chin is above the bar.
3. Pause at the top, squeezing your back muscles.
4. Lower yourself back down in a controlled manner until your arms are fully extended.
