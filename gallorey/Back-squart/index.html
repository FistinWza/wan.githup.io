Barbell Squats Guide
1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, keeping your back straight.
2. Position the barbell across your upper back and grip it firmly.
3. Lower your body by bending your knees and hips until your thighs are parallel to the ground.
4. Push through your heels to return to the starting position.
