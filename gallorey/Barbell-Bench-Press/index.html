Barbell Bench Press Guide
1. Lie on a flat bench with your feet firmly on the ground.
2. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width and unrack it.
3. Lower the bar to your chest in a controlled motion.
4. Press the bar back up until your arms are fully extended.
