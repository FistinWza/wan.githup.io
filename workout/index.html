Additional Notes

  • ✅ Progressive Overload – Increase weight or reps gradually.
  • ✅ Rest Periods: 60-90 sec for hypertrophy, 2-3 min for strength.
  • ✅ Core Training (Optional on Rest Days): Hanging leg raises, planks, ab rollouts.
  • ✅ Cardio (Optional on Rest Days): 10-20 min of incline walking, cycling, or jump rope.
