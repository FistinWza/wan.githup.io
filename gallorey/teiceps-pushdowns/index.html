Triceps Rope Pushdowns Guide
1. Attach a rope handle to a high pulley cable machine and grasp it with a neutral grip.
2. Stand with a slight forward lean and tuck your elbows close to your sides.
3. Push the rope downward until your arms are fully extended.
4. Squeeze your triceps at the bottom, then slowly return to the starting position.
